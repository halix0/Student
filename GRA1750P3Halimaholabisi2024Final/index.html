Carrot Bliss Buns

Welcome to Whisk N' Wonder by Halimah Olabisi. This is your ultimate destination for delightful carrot buns that blend flavor and wellness together. Our recipe celebrates the natural sweetness of carrots, transforming them into soft, fluffy buns perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

Makes about 16-20 buns

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup grated carrots (about 2 medium carrots)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup milk (or plant-based alternative)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Baking Instructions

  1. Prepare the Mixture: Blend grated carrots, milk, and eggs. In another bowl, whisk together dry ingredients. Combine both mixtures, then fold in melted butter, vanilla, and optional extras.
  2. Heat the Oil: In a deep frying pan, heat enough oil over medium heat.
  3. Shape the Buns: Portion the dough into small rounds or patties.
  4. Fry the Buns: Carefully fry for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Drain: Remove and place on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  6. Serve: Enjoy warm buns with your favorite dipping sauce!
